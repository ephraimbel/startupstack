**Objective**: Re-engage dormant users and prevent churn

## Email Design and Branding

### Email Template Design
- Clean, mobile-first design
- UGCContracts brand colors (Trust Blue #3B82F6, Success Green #22C55E)
- Professional but approachable typography
- Clear, prominent CTA buttons
- Creator testimonial callout boxes
- Social media icons and community links

### Personalization Elements
- First name in subject lines and content
- Contract creation progress and milestones
- Deal value tracking and protection metrics
- Relevant content based on creator niche/platform
- Geographic personalization for legal considerations

### Email Best Practices
- Mobile-optimized for creator audience (80% mobile usage)
- Clear, action-oriented subject lines
- One primary CTA per email
- Social proof in every email (testimonials, metrics, community size)
- Educational value in every message
- Personal tone from founder, not corporate messaging

## Automation Triggers

### Behavioral Triggers
- **First contract created**: Trigger congratulatory email + upgrade sequence
- **Multiple contracts in free tier**: Trigger upgrade sequence
- **Contract signed by brand**: Trigger success celebration email
- **Account inactive 30 days**: Trigger win-back sequence
- **Subscription cancelled**: Trigger retention sequence

### Milestone Triggers
- **5 contracts created**: Trigger "power user" recognition email
- **$10K+ deals protected**: Trigger success celebration with social share prompt
- **90 days as customer**: Trigger loyalty email with special perks
- **Referral successful**: Trigger thank you email with rewards

## Success Metrics and KPIs

### Email Performance Metrics
- **Open Rate**: Target 35%+ (creator audience is highly engaged)
- **Click Rate**: Target 8%+ (educational content drives clicks)
- **Conversion Rate**: Target 15%+ for upgrade sequences
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Keep under 2% (high-value content retention)

### Business Impact Metrics
- **Onboarding Completion**: 70%+ create first contract within 7 days
- **Free to Paid Conversion**: 15%+ upgrade within 30 days
- **Customer Retention**: 90%+ retention at 90 days
- **Referral Generation**: 25%+ of customers refer others

### A/B Testing Plan
- Subject line optimization (emotional vs. rational appeals)
- CTA button text and positioning
- Email frequency for different segments
- Content mix (educational vs. promotional ratio)
- Personalization depth and relevance

## Technical Implementation

### Email Service Provider
- **Recommended**: ConvertKit or Mailchimp
- **Required Features**: Automation workflows, segmentation, A/B testing
- **Integrations**: Supabase for user data, Stripe for subscription status

### List Segmentation
- **Free Tier Users**: Different messaging than paid users
- **Creator Plan Users**: Feature spotlights and community content  
- **Pro Users**: Advanced tips and exclusive content
- **Brand Users**: B2B focused content and case studies
- **Inactive Users**: Win-back campaigns and re-engagement

Generate a comprehensive email marketing system that nurtures UGCContracts users from signup to successful customer while building long-term engagement through educational value and community building.
