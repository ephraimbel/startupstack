# UGCContracts User Personas - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create detailed user personas for UGCContracts, focusing on UGC creators and brand managers who need professional contract solutions for creator-brand partnerships.

## Primary Personas

### 1. Creator Persona: "Ashley Rodriguez" - The Professional UGC Creator

**Demographics**:
- Age: 27
- Location: Austin, Texas
- Occupation: Full-time UGC Creator (former marketing coordinator)
- Income: $85K annually from brand partnerships
- Education: Bachelor's in Marketing

**Creator Stats**:
- Instagram: 125K followers
- TikTok: 89K followers
- YouTube: 32K subscribers
- Niche: Beauty and lifestyle content
- Monthly brand deals: 8-12 collaborations
- Deal value range: $800-$3,000 per campaign

**Goals & Motivations**:
- Protect herself from payment disputes and usage rights violations
- Look more professional when working with brands
- Save time on contract negotiations and admin work
- Scale her creator business without hiring expensive legal help
- Build long-term relationships with reputable brands
- Ensure fair compensation for her content and time

**Pain Points & Frustrations**:
- Has been burned twice by brands using content beyond agreed terms
- Spent $1,500 on a lawyer for one contract dispute
- Brands often want to negotiate over DMs which takes hours
- Generic contract templates don't cover UGC-specific scenarios
- Tracking multiple deals and their terms is overwhelming
- Afraid to push back on unfair terms and lose opportunities

**Behavior & Habits**:
- Creates content 6 days a week
- Spends 2-3 hours daily on brand outreach and negotiations
- Active in creator Facebook groups and Discord communities
- Uses Canva, Later, and various analytics tools
- Primarily communicates with brands via Instagram DMs and email
- Reviews contracts on her phone between content creation

**Technology Comfort**: High - comfortable with creator tools, scheduling platforms, and basic legal documents

**How They Currently Handle Contracts**:
- Uses a basic template she found in a creator Facebook group
- Modifies it manually for each deal (takes 30-45 minutes)
- Often skips contracts for smaller deals due to time constraints
- Has had 3 payment disputes in the past year
- Uses DocuSign for signatures when brands require it

**What Would Make Them Love UGCContracts**:
- Generates complete contracts in under 2 minutes
- Covers all UGC-specific terms automatically
- Makes her look professional to brand partners
- Prevents payment and usage disputes
- Mobile-friendly for on-the-go contract management
- Reasonable pricing that doesn't eat into deal profits

**Quotes**:
- "I know I should use contracts for everything, but it takes so long to customize them for each deal"
- "I've been burned by brands using my content in ways I never agreed to"
- "I want to look professional, but I can't afford a lawyer for every partnership"

- Works with 25-30 creators monthly
- Spends $150K annually on influencer partnerships
- Team: David + 1 coordinator + external legal counsel

**Goals & Motivations**:
- Streamline creator partnership process
- Ensure proper usage rights for paid advertising
- Reduce legal costs and contract negotiation time
- Build long-term relationships with reliable creators
- Scale influencer program without proportional admin overhead

**Pain Points & Frustrations**:
- Each creator has different contract preferences and templates
- Legal team charges $300-500 per contract review
- Usage rights confusion has led to 2 creator disputes this year

- Some creators don't use contracts, creating compliance risk
- Difficult to standardize terms across different creator types

**Behavior & Habits**:
- Manages creator partnerships through spreadsheets and Slack
- Reviews 15-20 creator applications weekly
- Coordinates with legal team monthly for contract reviews
- Uses Grin for creator discovery but manual for contracts
- Prefers email communication for formal agreements

**Technology Comfort**: High - uses marketing tools, CRM systems, and collaboration platforms

**How They Currently Handle Contracts**:
- Has legal team create brand-friendly template
- Manually customizes for each creator partnership
- Uses DocuSign for signatures
- Tracks agreements in spreadsheets
- Legal review adds 3-5 days to partnership timeline

**What Would Make Them Love UGCContracts**:
- Consistent, brand-protective contracts across all creators
- Reduced legal review needs and costs
- Faster partnership execution
- Built-in tracking and renewal alerts
- Creator adoption reduces negotiation friction
- Fair terms that attract quality creators

**Quotes**:
- "We want to protect our brand, but we also want creators to feel the terms are fair"
- "Legal review is our biggest bottleneck in scaling creator partnerships"
- "Some of our best creators actually prefer working with brands that use professional contracts"
