# SponsorCalc - Customer Interview Guide

Design customer interview framework to validate product-market fit and gather insights for SponsorCalc development.

## Interview Objectives
- Validate pain points around sponsorship pricing
- Understand current pricing methods and tools
- Test concept and feature prioritization  
- Gather user story details and workflows
- Identify price sensitivity and willingness to pay

## Target Interview Segments
- Micro creators (10K-50K followers)
- Mid-tier creators (50K-250K followers)  
- Top creators (250K+ followers)
- Talent managers and agencies

## Interview Guide Framework
Include structured questions, follow-up prompts, and validation techniques for each customer segment.
