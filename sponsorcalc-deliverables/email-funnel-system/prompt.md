# SponsorCalc - Email Funnel System

Create complete email marketing funnel system for SponsorCalc user acquisition and conversion.

## Funnel Objectives
- Convert visitors to free users
- Activate free users to generate rate cards
- Convert free users to paid subscribers
- Retain and expand existing customers

## Funnel Stages
1. **Awareness**: Educational content and lead magnets
2. **Interest**: Free tool trial and value demonstration
3. **Consideration**: Feature comparison and social proof
4. **Conversion**: Upgrade prompts and limited offers
5. **Retention**: Ongoing value and feature adoption

## Email Types
- Welcome sequences for new signups
- Educational content series
- Product feature spotlights
- Conversion and upgrade campaigns
- Re-engagement for inactive users

Include complete email copy, automation triggers, and performance optimization strategies.
