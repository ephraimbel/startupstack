# SponsorCalc - Sales Funnel

Design comprehensive sales funnel for SponsorCalc free-to-paid conversion optimization.

## Funnel Overview
**Model**: Product-led growth with freemium conversion
**Primary Goal**: Convert free users to Creator plan ($19/month)
**Secondary Goal**: Upgrade Creator users to Pro plan ($49/month)

## Funnel Stages

### 1. Awareness (Top of Funnel)
- SEO content marketing
- Social media presence  
- Creator community engagement
- Paid advertising campaigns

### 2. Interest (Lead Generation)
- Free rate calculation tool
- Educational content consumption
- Email list signups via lead magnets
- Social media follows and engagement

### 3. Trial/Activation (Product Experience)
- Free account creation
- Platform connection and data sync
- First rate card generation
- Feature exploration and education

### 4. Conversion (Upgrade Decision)
- PDF export paywall encounter
- Feature limitation awareness
- ROI demonstration and case studies
- Upgrade prompts and incentives

### 5. Expansion (Account Growth)
- Plan upgrades (Creator to Pro)
- Feature adoption and utilization
- Referral program participation
- Long-term retention strategies

Include A/B testing strategies, funnel analytics, and optimization tactics for each stage.
