# SponsorCalc - Ad Creatives

Create comprehensive ad creative assets for SponsorCalc, a creator sponsorship pricing intelligence platform.

## Core Message
"Know your worth. Get data-backed sponsorship rates in seconds."

## Brand Positioning
For content creators tired of guessing what to charge, SponsorCalc is an AI-powered pricing platform that generates data-backed rate cards based on actual performance.

## Key Selling Points
- Data-backed rates (not follower count)
- Professional PDF rate cards
- Negotiation scripts and tactics
- Multi-platform support (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, podcasts)
- Industry benchmarks and comparisons

## Target Audiences
1. **Primary**: Content creators with 10K-500K followers doing 2+ brand deals per month
2. **Secondary**: Talent agencies and influencer marketing managers

## Ad Creative Variations

### Social Media Ads (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook)
Create 5 ad variations:

1. **Problem-focused**: "Brands lowball you because you don't know market rates"
2. **Solution-focused**: "Generate professional rate cards in seconds"
3. **Benefit-focused**: "Creators increase rates by 20-40% with data-backed pricing"
4. **Social proof**: "Join thousands of creators who stopped leaving money on the table"
5. **Free trial**: "Calculate your rates free - no card required"

### Display Ads (Google, programmatic)
Create banner variations for:
- 728x90 (leaderboard)
- 300x250 (medium rectangle)
- 320x50 (mobile banner)

### Video Ads (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube)
Create 15-30 second video concepts:
1. "Rate reveal" showing before/after sponsorship prices
2. Screen recording of rate card generation
3. Creator testimonial format
4. Split-screen: "Guessing vs. Knowing your worth"

## Brand Guidelines
- **Colors**: Primary #10B981 (emerald green), Secondary #1F2937 (slate), Accent #F59E0B (gold)
- **Tone**: Confident and supportive, empowering
- **Voice**: Like a savvy friend who has your back
- **Fonts**: Satoshi/Clash Display (headlines), Inter (body)

## Call-to-Actions
- "Calculate My Rates Free"
- "Know Your Worth"
- "Stop Guessing, Start Knowing"
- "Get My Rate Card"
- "See What I'm Worth"

## Success Metrics to Highlight
- "Increase rates by 20-40%"
- "Professional rate cards ready to send"
- "Data from 500M+ creator profiles analyzed"
- "Works with all major platforms"

Include copy, design direction, and asset specifications for each creative type.
