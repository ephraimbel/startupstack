# SponsorCalc - Content Calendar

Create a comprehensive content marketing calendar for SponsorCalc focused on educating creators about sponsorship pricing and driving organic growth.

## Content Pillars

### 1. Education (40%)
- Sponsorship pricing fundamentals
- Platform-specific rate strategies
- Creator business development
- Negotiation techniques and scripts

### 2. Product (30%)
- Feature spotlights and tutorials
- Rate card examples and templates
- Success stories and case studies
- Behind-the-scenes product development

### 3. Industry (20%)
- Creator economy trends and news
- Platform updates affecting monetization
- Sponsorship market analysis
- Competitive landscape insights

### 4. Community (10%)
- Creator spotlights and interviews
- User-generated content features
- Community challenges and campaigns
- Team and company culture content

## Platform-Specific Strategy

### Blog/SEO Content
**Publishing**: 2 posts per week
**Topics**: In-depth guides, industry analysis, tool comparisons
**Goal**: Organic search traffic and thought leadership

### Twitter
**Publishing**: Daily engagement, 5-7 original posts/week
**Topics**: Quick tips, industry commentary, community engagement
**Goal**: Creator community building and brand awareness

### LinkedIn
**Publishing**: 3 posts per week
**Topics**: B2B content for agencies, industry insights, professional advice
**Goal**: Agency and enterprise audience development

### TikTok/Instagram Reels
**Publishing**: 3-4 videos per week
**Topics**: Quick tips, rate reveals, creator education
**Goal**: Creator audience growth and platform native content

### YouTube
**Publishing**: 1 video per week (post-MVP)
**Topics**: Deep-dive tutorials, creator interviews, industry analysis
**Goal**: Authority building and long-form education

Include detailed monthly themes, weekly topics, and content production workflows for each platform.
