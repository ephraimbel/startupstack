# SponsorCalc - Email Sequences

Design automated email sequences for user onboarding, engagement, and conversion optimization.

## Welcome Sequence (New Signups)

### Email 1: Welcome & First Steps (Immediate)
**Subject**: "Welcome to SponsorCalc! Let's get your rates calculated"
**Goal**: Drive platform connection and first rate calculation

### Email 2: Success Tips (Day 2)
**Subject**: "The #1 mistake creators make with sponsorship pricing"
**Goal**: Education and engagement, drive return visit

### Email 3: Professional Output (Day 5)
**Subject**: "Why brands don't take your rates seriously (+ how to fix it)"
**Goal**: Highlight PDF export feature, drive upgrade consideration

### Email 4: Social Proof (Day 8)
**Subject**: "How Maya increased her rates by 35% (real story)"
**Goal**: Showcase success stories and build confidence

### Email 5: Upgrade Offer (Day 12)
**Subject**: "Ready to send professional rate cards to brands?"
**Goal**: Direct upgrade prompt with special offer

## Engagement Sequences

### Re-engagement (Inactive Users)
- Day 7: "Missing out on better rates? Your account is waiting"
- Day 14: "Last chance: See what you're really worth"
- Day 30: "We miss you! Here's what's new at SponsorCalc"

### Feature Adoption
- Negotiation Scripts: "Turn brand objections into yes (free scripts inside)"
- Deal Tracking: "Stop losing track of your sponsorship deals"
- Benchmarks: "See how your rates compare to similar creators"

## Conversion Optimization

### Free to Paid
- Upgrade prompts based on usage patterns
- Feature-specific conversion emails
- Limited-time offers and incentives
- ROI-focused messaging

### Plan Upgrades
- Creator to Pro upgrade sequences
- Agency tier targeting for multi-creator users
- Feature usage triggers for appropriate upgrades

Include detailed copy, timing, and personalization strategies for each sequence type.
