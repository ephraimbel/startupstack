# SponsorCalc - Landing Page

Create a high-converting landing page for SponsorCalc that turns visitors into users and users into paying customers.

## Page Objective
Convert content creators struggling with sponsorship pricing into SponsorCalc users, with emphasis on the free tier to reduce friction.

## Target Audience
**Primary**: Content creators with 10K-500K followers across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or podcasts who do 2+ brand deals per month
**Secondary**: Talent agencies and influencer marketing managers

## Landing Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
**Headline**: "Stop guessing what to charge for sponsorships"
**Subheadline**: "Get data-backed rates based on your engagement, audience, and niche. Generate professional rate cards in seconds."

**CTAs**:
- Primary: "Calculate My Rates — Free"
- Secondary: "See example rate card →"

**Hero Visual**: Screenshot or animation of the rate card generation process

### 2. Problem Section
**Headline**: "Brands have the data. You're guessing."

**Pain Points**:
1. **"You have no idea what to charge"**
   - Detail: "Every creator asks the same question: 'Is this rate too high? Too low?' Without data, you're flying blind."

2. **"Brands lowball you constantly"**
   - Detail: "They know what other creators charge. You don't. Guess who wins that negotiation?"

3. **"You can't justify your rates"**
   - Detail: "When brands push back, you fold. You don't have the data to stand your ground."

### 3. Solution Section
**Headline**: "Data-backed rates you can defend"

**Features Grid**:
1. **Connect Your Accounts**
   - Description: "We pull your real engagement, demographics, and growth. No fake numbers."
   - Icon: Link/connect symbol

2. **AI-Powered Rate Calculation**
   - Description: "Our algorithm factors engagement, niche CPMs, audience value, and market rates."
   - Icon: Calculator/AI brain

3. **Professional Rate Cards**
   - Description: "Export beautiful PDF rate cards ready to send to any brand."
   - Icon: Document/card

4. **Tiered Pricing**
   - Description: "Rates for every deliverable: posts, stories, videos, bundles. Low to premium tiers."
   - Icon: Layers/tiers

5. **Negotiation Scripts**
   - Description: "Know exactly what to say when brands push back on your rates."
   - Icon: Message bubble

6. **Industry Benchmarks**
   - Description: "See how your rates compare to creators in your niche and follower range."
   - Icon: Chart/graph

### 4. How It Works
**Headline**: "Know your worth in 3 steps"

**Steps**:
1. **Connect your platforms** - "Link Instagram, YouTube, TikTok—wherever you create."
2. **Get your rates** - "AI analyzes your data and generates personalized pricing."
3. **Send your rate card** - "Export a professional PDF and negotiate with confidence."

### 5. Social Proof
**Headline**: "Creators are getting paid more"

**Elements**:
- Creator testimonials (focus on rate increases, successful negotiations)
- Usage statistics ("XX,XXX rate cards generated", "$XXM in sponsorship value calculated")
- Platform logos (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok)

### 6. Pricing Section
**Headline**: "Know your worth—starting free"
**Subheadline**: "Basic rate calculation is free forever. Upgrade for pro features."

**Pricing Tiers**:
1. **Free** - $0
   - 1 platform connection
   - Basic rate calculation
   - Simple rate display (no PDF)
   - Community benchmarks

2. **Creator** - $19/month
   - 3 platforms
   - Professional PDF export
   - Negotiation scripts
   - Industry benchmarks
   - Deal tracker

3. **Pro** - $49/month
   - Unlimited platforms
   - Custom branded media kit
   - AI-personalized scripts
   - Rate trend tracking

### 7. FAQ Section
**Common Questions**:

1. **"How do you calculate my rates?"**
   - "We analyze your engagement rate (not just followers), audience demographics, your niche's typical CPMs, and comparable creator rates."

2. **"What platforms do you support?"**
   - "Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok with direct API connection. Podcasts, newsletters, and other platforms via manual entry."

3. **"Why should I trust these rates?"**
   - "Our rates are based on real market data, not arbitrary multipliers. We continuously update our benchmarks."

4. **"What if a brand says my rate is too high?"**
   - "That's what negotiation scripts are for. We give you word-for-word responses to common objections."

5. **"Is my data safe?"**
   - "We only access public metrics you'd share anyway. We never post on your behalf or share individual data."

### 8. Final CTA Section
**Headline**: "Your next brand deal should pay what you're worth"
**Subheadline**: "Join thousands of creators who stopped leaving money on the table."
**CTA**: "Calculate My Rates Free"

## Design Guidelines

### Visual Elements
- Show actual rate card examples prominently
- Use before/after pricing comparisons
- Include creator photos (diverse, authentic)
- Data visualizations (charts showing rate improvements)
- Mobile-responsive design (creators primarily on mobile)

### Color Usage
- Primary CTA buttons: Emerald green (#10B981)
- Secondary elements: Gold accent (#F59E0B)
- Text: Slate (#1F2937) on light backgrounds
- Backgrounds: Clean whites and light grays

### Typography
- Headlines: Satoshi/Clash Display (confident, modern)
- Body text: Inter (readable, professional)
- Large, scannable sections
- Bullet points over paragraphs

## Conversion Optimization

### Trust Signals
- Creator testimonials with real names/handles
- "No credit card required" messaging
- Security badges and data protection info
- Number of creators served

### Friction Reduction
- Prominent free tier
- Clear pricing (no "Contact us")
- Simple signup flow
- Mobile-optimized experience

### Urgency/Scarcity
- "Join X creators already using SponsorCalc"
- "Don't lose money on your next deal"
- Time-based benefits ("Get your rate card in 60 seconds")

## SEO Considerations

### Target Keywords
- "sponsorship pricing calculator"
- "influencer rate card"
- "how much to charge for sponsorships"
- "creator pricing tool"
- "brand deal rates"

### Meta Tags
- Title: "SponsorCalc — Know What to Charge for Sponsorships"
- Description: "Get data-backed sponsorship rates based on your engagement and niche. Generate professional rate cards and negotiate with confidence."

The landing page should feel professional enough that creators would confidently share it with major brands, while being approachable enough for smaller creators to feel welcome.
