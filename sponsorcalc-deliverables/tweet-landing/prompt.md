# SponsorCalc - Tweet Landing Page

Create optimized landing page specifically designed for Twitter/X traffic and social media referrals.

## Page Objectives
- Convert social media traffic to free signups
- Communicate value proposition quickly
- Drive immediate platform connection and rate calculation
- Optimize for mobile-first audience

## Design Requirements
- Mobile-responsive design (90% of Twitter traffic)
- Fast loading speed (<2 seconds)
- Clear social proof and credibility signals
- Minimal form fields and friction
- Strong visual hierarchy and scannable content

## Content Structure
1. **Hero Section**: Immediate value prop and CTA
2. **Social Proof**: Twitter testimonials and creator success stories  
3. **Quick Demo**: Visual showing rate calculation process
4. **Free Trial**: Prominent signup with "no card required"
5. **FAQ**: Address common objections quickly

## Twitter-Specific Optimizations
- Thread-friendly messaging and format
- Creator economy language and terminology
- Social sharing optimization
- Mobile-first design and interactions

## A/B Testing Elements
- Headlines and value propositions
- CTA button copy and placement
- Social proof presentation
- Form fields and signup flow

Include copy, design mockups, and conversion optimization strategies specifically for social media traffic.
