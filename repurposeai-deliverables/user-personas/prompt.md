# RepurposeAI User Personas - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create detailed user personas for RepurposeAI based on content creator segments, their pain points, goals, and buying behavior.

## Research Foundation
**Data Sources**: Creator surveys, customer interviews, market research, competitor analysis
**Validation**: Based on real user behavior and feedback from 12,000+ creators using RepurposeAI

## Primary Personas

### Persona 1: Creator Casey (Primary Target)
**Demographics**:
- Age: 25-35
- Location: US/Canada/UK  
- Income: $40K-$80K (mix of AdSense, sponsors, products)
- Education: College degree
- Platform: YouTube (50K-200K subscribers)

**Psychographics**:
- Values creative freedom and authenticity
- Growth-minded but time-constrained
- Tech-savvy but not developer
- Community-focused

**Goals**:
- Grow audience across multiple platforms without burnout
- Land bigger sponsorship deals by showing multi-platform presence  
- Build sustainable creator business, not just hobby

**Pain Points**:
- Spending all creative energy on one video, then manual repurposing takes hours
- Seeing other creators everywhere but not understanding how they have time
- Knowing should be on TikTok/Twitter/LinkedIn but can't keep up

**Content Behavior**:
- Publishes 1 YouTube video per week
- Sporadically posts on other platforms
- Spends 4+ hours per video on creation, 2-4 hours on repurposing (when they do it)

**Buying Behavior**:
- Research process: Asks creator friends, checks Twitter, watches YouTube reviews
- Decision factors: Time saved vs cost, quality of output, ease of use
- Budget: $30-100/month for tools that clearly save time

**Quote**: "I know my content could reach way more people. I just don't have time to be a one-person media company."

### Persona 2: Podcaster Pete (Secondary Target)  
**Demographics**:
- Age: 30-45
- Location: US metro areas
- Income: $80K-$150K (day job + podcast revenue)
- Education: MBA or equivalent
- Platform: Business/tech podcast (1K-10K downloads/episode)

**Psychographics**:
- Strategic thinker focused on ROI
- Time-poor due to day job + podcast
- Quality over quantity mindset
- Professional reputation conscious

**Goals**:
- Turn podcast into lead generation for consulting/business
- Build thought leadership presence on LinkedIn  
- Grow downloads and attract bigger guests

**Pain Points**:
- Amazing conversations disappear after episode airs
- No time to write LinkedIn posts or newsletters consistently
- Feels like leaving money on table with unused content

**Content Behavior**:
- Records 1-2 episodes per week
- Knows should do more with each episode but time is impossible
- Occasionally shares clips manually but inconsistent

**Buying Behavior**:
- Research process: LinkedIn recommendations, Google searches, asks mastermind group
- Decision factors: ROI calculation, professionalism of output, integration with workflow
- Budget: $50-200/month viewed as business investment

**Quote**: "Every episode has 10 pieces of content in it. I just can't find the time to pull them out."

### Persona 3: Course Creator Carla (Growth Target)
**Demographics**:
- Age: 35-50
- Location: US/UK/Australia  
- Income: $150K-$500K from courses/coaching
- Education: Expert in their field
- Platforms: Multiple (YouTube, email list, social media)

**Psychographics**:
- Entrepreneurial with scaling mindset
- Systems and automation focused
- Premium brand positioning
- Community and impact driven

**Goals**:
- Fill course launches with consistent content marketing
- Build omnipresence to support premium pricing
- Maximize value from webinars and training content

**Pain Points**:
- Sitting on hours of valuable training content not being used
- Launch periods require content sprints they can't sustain
- Hiring content team is expensive and inconsistent

**Content Behavior**:
- Creates extensive course content and webinars
- Inconsistent on social media between launches
- Has team but struggles with content repurposing coordination

**Buying Behavior**:
- Research process: Asks in mastermind groups, follows industry leaders' recommendations
- Decision factors: ROI clearly demonstrable, team features, professional brand standards  
- Budget: $100-500/month, willing to pay for quality and team features

**Quote**: "I have 100 hours of training content. If I could turn that into a year of social content, I'd never worry about marketing again."

## Persona Usage Guide

### Marketing Applications
- **Ad Targeting**: Use demographics and psychographics for precise audience targeting
- **Messaging**: Tailor value propositions to each persona's specific goals and pain points
- **Content Strategy**: Create content that resonates with each persona's interests and challenges

### Product Development  
- **Feature Prioritization**: Build features that solve the biggest problems for primary personas
- **User Experience**: Design workflows that match each persona's technical comfort level
- **Pricing Strategy**: Align pricing tiers with each persona's budget and value perception

### Customer Success
- **Onboarding**: Customize onboarding flows for each persona type
- **Support**: Train team to recognize and address persona-specific concerns  
- **Retention**: Develop retention strategies based on each persona's success metrics

Generate detailed personas that drive better targeting, messaging, and product decisions across all customer touchpoints.
