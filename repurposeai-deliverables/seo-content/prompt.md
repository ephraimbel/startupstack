# RepurposeAI SEO Content - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create a comprehensive SEO content strategy for RepurposeAI targeting high-intent keywords in the content creation and repurposing space.

## SEO Strategy
**Goal**: Capture organic search traffic from creators looking for repurposing solutions
**Target**: 10,000+ monthly organic visitors within 6 months
**Approach**: Informational content that demonstrates expertise, then converts to trials

1. **"content repurposing tool"** (1,900/month)

   - **Title**: "11 Best Content Repurposing Tools in 2024 (Free & Paid)"
   - **Angle**: Comprehensive review with RepurposeAI featured prominently

2. **"repurpose youtube videos"** (1,300/month)
   - **Content**: Complete how-to guide with step-by-step process
   - **Title**: "How to Repurpose YouTube Videos: Turn 1 Video Into 20+ Posts"
   - **Angle**: Educational content with RepurposeAI as the solution

3. **"turn video into social media posts"** (880/month)
   - **Content**: Framework and tools guide
   - **Title**: "How to Turn Any Video Into Social Media Content (5-Step Process)"
   - **Angle**: Process explanation with tool recommendations
