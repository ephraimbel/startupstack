# RepurposeAI Sales Funnel - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Design a complete sales funnel for RepurposeAI that takes prospects from awareness through trial to paying customer, optimized for creator psychology and buying behavior.

## Funnel Strategy
**Target**: Content creators struggling with multi-platform presence
**Goal**: 15% trial-to-paid conversion rate
**Approach**: Education-first, value demonstration, social proof

## Funnel Stages

### Stage 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)
**Traffic Sources**: 
- Organic content (Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn)
- Paid ads (Meta, Google, LinkedIn)
- Creator partnerships and affiliates

**Content Types**:
- Educational blog posts about content repurposing
- YouTube tutorials on content multiplication
- Twitter threads about creator productivity
- Podcast guest appearances

**Conversion Goal**: Email list signup via lead magnet

### Stage 2: Interest (Middle of Funnel)  
**Touchpoints**:
- Lead magnet delivery and follow-up emails
- Retargeting ads to website visitors
- Social proof content and case studies

**Content Types**:
- Email sequence demonstrating framework
- Demo videos showing product in action
- Customer success stories and testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes content building trust

**Conversion Goal**: Free trial signup

### Stage 3: Consideration (Trial Period)
**Touchpoints**:
- Product onboarding and activation emails
- In-app guidance and tooltips
- Customer success outreach for high-value users

**Content Types**:
- Onboarding email sequence
- Feature demonstration and tips
- Usage milestone celebrations
- Upgrade prompts for power users

**Conversion Goal**: Paid subscription

### Stage 4: Retention (Post-Purchase)
**Touchpoints**:
- Customer success emails and check-ins  
- Feature announcement and education
- Community building and user-generated content

**Content Types**:
- Advanced feature tutorials
- Customer spotlight and case studies
- Referral program invitations
- Upgrade path education for higher tiers

**Conversion Goal**: Long-term retention and expansion

Generate this funnel with clear conversion pathways, compelling content at each stage, and measurable outcomes that drive sustainable growth.
