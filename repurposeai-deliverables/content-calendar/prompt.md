# RepurposeAI Content Calendar - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create a comprehensive 90-day content marketing calendar for RepurposeAI that positions the brand as the go-to content repurposing solution for creators while building authority in the creator economy space.

## Content Strategy
**Goal**: Drive awareness, build authority, generate trials
**North Star**: Position RepurposeAI as essential tool for serious creators
**Approach**: Educational content that shows, rather than tells

## Content Pillars (Weight Distribution)

### Pillar 1: Content Multiplication Strategy (35%)
**Purpose**: Teach creators how to think about repurposing strategically
**Content Types**: How-to threads, strategy guides, framework posts
**Example Topics**:
- "The 1-to-20 content framework"  
- "Why most creators waste 90% of their content value"
- "Platform-native vs cross-posting: The difference"

### Pillar 2: Platform-Specific Tactics (30%)
**Purpose**: Deep dive into what works on each platform
**Content Types**: Platform guides, algorithm insights, format breakdowns
**Example Topics**:
- "Anatomy of viral Twitter threads"
- "LinkedIn vs Instagram: Same content, different angle"
- "TikTok hooks that actually stop the scroll"

### Pillar 3: Creator Economy Trends (20%)  
**Purpose**: Industry insights and thought leadership
**Content Types**: Trend analysis, predictions, data insights
**Example Topics**:
- "Algorithm changes and what they mean"
- "Creator monetization trends for 2024"
- "The rise of multi-platform presence"

### Pillar 4: Product & Customer Stories (15%)
**Purpose**: Social proof and product awareness  
**Content Types**: Customer spotlights, feature announcements, behind-the-scenes
**Example Topics**:
- "How [Creator] went from 1 to 5 platforms"
- "New feature: AI brand voice training"
- "Building RepurposeAI: Lessons learned"
