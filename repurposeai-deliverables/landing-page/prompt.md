- **Colors**: Primary violet (#7C3AED), accent orange (#F97316), clean whites/grays
- **Typography**: Cal Sans for headlines, Inter for body text

- **Load Time**: <3 seconds on 3G
- **Core Web Vitals**: LCP <2.5s, FID <100ms, CLS <0.1

- **CTA Placement**: Above fold, after problem, after social proof, final CTA
