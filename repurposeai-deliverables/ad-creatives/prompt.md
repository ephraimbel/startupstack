# RepurposeAI Ad Creatives - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create high-converting ad creative concepts for RepurposeAI across Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, LinkedIn, and Twitter. Focus on creator pain points and transformation stories.

- **Primary**: YouTubers, podcasters, course creators with 10K+ following
- **Secondary**: Agencies managing multiple creators

## Creative Requirements

### Technical Specs
- **Meta**: 1:1, 4:5, 16:9 ratios. Video <4MB
- **Google**: Responsive display. 1.91:1, 1:1, 4:5 ratios  
- **LinkedIn**: 1.91:1 for sponsored content
- **Twitter**: 1.91:1 or 1:1. Video <1GB

### Brand Guidelines
- Colors: Primary violet (#7C3AED), accent orange (#F97316)
- Fonts: Inter for ads, consistent with brand
- Logo: Include RepurposeAI logo in corner
- Tone: Energetic, creator-friendly, benefit-focused

### Compliance Notes
- Include "Sponsored" or "Ad" labels where required
- No misleading claims about earnings
- Honor platform advertising policies
- Include disclaimers for free trials

Generate these creatives with compelling hooks that stop the scroll, clear value propositions, and strong CTAs that drive free trial signups.
