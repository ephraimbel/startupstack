# RepurposeAI Pricing Strategy - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create a comprehensive pricing strategy for RepurposeAI that maximizes revenue while ensuring accessibility for target creator segments.

## Pricing Philosophy
**Value-Based Pricing**: Price based on value delivered (time saved, content multiplied) rather than cost-plus
**Creator-Friendly**: Accessible entry point with clear upgrade paths
**Usage-Aligned**: Pricing scales with creator content volume and needs

## Market Analysis

### Competitive Landscape
- **Repurpose.io**: $25-125/month (automation-focused)
- **Opus Clip**: $19-99/month (video clips only)  
- **Descript**: $24-44/month (editing tool with repurposing)
- **Manual Alternative**: $2,000-5,000/month (hiring editors/VAs)

### Value Proposition Anchoring
**Primary Anchor**: Replaces $3K/month editor for $79/month
**Time Anchor**: Saves 40+ hours/month (worth $800+ at $20/hour)
**Growth Anchor**: Multi-platform presence drives 3x faster audience growth

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter - $29/month
**Target Customer**: Part-time creators, testing the tool, lower volume

**Features**:
- 5 videos per month
- All content types (threads, posts, clips, newsletters)
- Basic templates and editing
- Email support
- 14-day free trial

**Value Communication**: "Less than $1 per video transformed into 20+ posts"
**Conversion Strategy**: Easy entry point, upgrade when hitting limits
**Expected Mix**: 30% of customers

### Creator - $79/month ⭐ Most Popular
**Target Customer**: Serious creators publishing weekly, growing actively

**Features**:
- 20 videos per month  
- Everything in Starter
- Brand voice training
- Custom templates
- Priority processing
- Content calendar view
- Priority email support

**Value Communication**: "Replace a $3K/month editor for $79/month"
**Positioning**: Sweet spot for serious creators
**Expected Mix**: 50% of customers

### Pro - $199/month
**Target Customer**: Full-time creators, agencies, course creators

**Features**:
- Unlimited videos
- Everything in Creator
- Team workspaces (3 seats)
- API access
- White-label exports
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom AI training

**Value Communication**: "Scale without limits, built for businesses"
**Upsell Strategy**: Target high-usage Creator users
**Expected Mix**: 20% of customers

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring Strategy
1. **Show Pro first** on pricing page (anchor at $199)
2. **Creator feels reasonable** at $79 compared to Pro
3. **Compare to alternatives** ($3K editor, $2K VA)

### Decoy Effect
- Starter: 5 videos for $29 ($5.80 per video)
- Creator: 20 videos for $79 ($3.95 per video) ← Better value
- Pro: Unlimited for $199 (unlimited value)

### Loss Aversion
- **Trial expiration**: "Don't lose your content setup"
- **Usage limits**: "You're approaching your video limit"
- **Feature access**: "Upgrade to keep advanced features"

## Discount Strategy

### Launch Promotions
**Early Bird**: 20% off first 3 months for first 1,000 customers
- Creates urgency and rewards early adopters
- Reduces price objections during launch
- Limited time maintains value perception

### Annual Pricing
**Discount**: 2 months free (17% off) for annual billing
- Improves cash flow and reduces churn
- Standard SaaS discount level
- Target: 30% of customers choose annual

### Special Discounts
**Student Creators**: 50% off with .edu email verification
**Non-Profit**: 30% off for registered non-profits
**Volume**: Custom pricing for 10+ seats

## Freemium vs Free Trial

### Chosen Model: Free Trial (14 days, no credit card)
**Rationale**:
- Video processing has real costs (AI, storage)
- Quality users more likely with trial commitment
- Clear upgrade path and urgency
- Prevents abuse and low-quality signups

**Trial Limitations**:
- 3 videos maximum during trial
- Watermarked exports (removed on paid)
- No priority processing
- Basic support only
