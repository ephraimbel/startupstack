# LaunchList Ad Creatives - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create comprehensive advertising creative briefs and copy for LaunchList, the viral waitlist platform, including social media ads, Google Ads, and content marketing assets targeting founders and indie hackers.

## Product Context
- **Core Value**: Viral waitlist platform that turns signups into marketers through referral mechanics
- **Target Audience**: Founders, indie hackers, product managers launching new products
- **Key Benefits**: Beautiful pages, built-in virality, 5-minute setup, free validation tier
- **Positioning**: "Waitlists that grow themselves" - modern alternative to boring email collection

## Platform-Specific Considerations

### Twitter/X Strategy
- Focus on founder success stories and viral metrics
- Use threads to break down viral launch strategies
- Engage with indie hacker community hashtags
- Share behind-the-scenes building content

### LinkedIn Strategy
- Professional tone emphasizing team efficiency
- Target product managers and startup leaders
- Focus on ROI and business metrics
- Case studies with specific results

### Product Hunt Strategy
- Emphasize launch-day excitement and community
- Focus on helping other makers succeed
- Share specific viral mechanics and tactics

## Success Metrics & Optimization

### Performance Targets
- **CTR**: 2-4% for social ads, 5-8% for search ads
- **Conversion Rate**: 8-15% landing page conversion
- **CPM**: <$5 for social, optimize for engagement
- **CPC**: <$2 for search, focus on high-intent keywords

### Optimization Strategy
- A/B testing on messaging and visuals every 2 weeks
- Audience refinement based on conversion data
- Creative refresh monthly to combat ad fatigue
- Landing page optimization for each ad campaign

This advertising strategy focuses on the founder community's preferred channels while emphasizing the unique viral mechanics that set LaunchList apart from generic email collection tools.
