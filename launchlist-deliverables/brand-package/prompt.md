# LaunchList Brand Package - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create a comprehensive brand identity system for LaunchList, a viral waitlist platform that helps founders create beautiful pre-launch pages with built-in referral mechanics to turn every signup into a marketer.

## Brand Positioning
- **Product**: Viral waitlist platform with drag-and-drop builder, referral tracking, email sequences, and analytics
- **Target Audience**: Founders, indie hackers, product managers launching new products who want to build pre-launch momentum
- **Core Value Prop**: "Waitlists that grow themselves" - beautiful pages with built-in virality, launch in minutes
- **Personality**: Energetic, builder-friendly, modern, confident, simple
