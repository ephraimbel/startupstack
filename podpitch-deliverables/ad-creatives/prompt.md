# PodPitch Ad Creatives - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create high-converting ad creatives for PodPitch across multiple platforms and formats, targeting founders and executives who want to build authority through podcast appearances but struggle with the outreach process.

## Brand & Positioning Context
- **Product**: AI-powered podcast outreach platform with 50K+ shows, personalized pitches, and pipeline management
- **Target**: Founders, consultants, B2B executives, authors, coaches seeking strategic podcast appearances
- **Value Prop**: "Get booked on podcasts that matter" - agency results without agency fees or loss of control
- **Brand Voice**: Professional, knowledgeable, direct - like a mentor who's mastered podcast booking

## Target Audience Insights

### Primary Audience (80% of ad spend)
- **Demographics**: 28-45 years old, 60% male, English-speaking, US/UK/CA/AU
- **Psychographics**: Ambitious, strategic, value efficiency and ROI
- **Pain Points**: Time-consuming research, generic pitches ignored, expensive agencies, missed opportunities
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn (primary), Google Search, business podcasts
- **Decision Factors**: ROI, professional credibility, time savings, control

### Secondary Audience (20% of ad spend)
- **Profile**: Authors, course creators, speakers launching products or building personal brands
- **Pain Points**: Need media coverage, limited PR budget, don't know where to start
- **Platforms**: Twitter, Facebook, YouTube (business channels)

## Creative Testing Framework

### Primary Variables to Test
1. **Value Props**: Cost savings vs. time savings vs. authority building
2. **Social Proof**: Success metrics vs. testimonials vs. case studies
3. **CTAs**: "Try free" vs. "Start pitching" vs. "See how it works"
4. **Positioning**: Agency alternative vs. DIY solution vs. professional tool
5. **Visuals**: Dashboards vs. people vs. results/metrics

### Creative Performance Indicators
**High-Performing Creative Characteristics**:
- Lead with specific, measurable benefits (save $3K/month, 18% response rate)
- Professional imagery that builds trust and credibility
- Clear before/after or problem/solution narrative
- Strong social proof from recognizable founders
- Specific, low-friction CTAs

**Testing Methodology**:
- A/B test one variable at a time for clean attribution
- Run tests for statistical significance (minimum 1,000 impressions per variant)
- Track full funnel: CTR → Landing page conversion → Free trial → Paid signup
- Refresh creative weekly, retire low performers after 2 weeks

## Creative Production Guidelines

### Visual Standards
- **Professional Aesthetic**: Clean, business-focused design that builds trust
- **Color Palette**: Teal (#0F766E) and Amber (#F59E0B) for brand consistency
- **Typography**: Professional fonts (DM Sans, Inter) that are easily readable
- **Imagery**: Real founders in business contexts, dashboard screenshots

### Content Guidelines
- **Tone**: Professional but not corporate, knowledgeable and direct
- **Proof Points**: Always include specific metrics (response rates, cost savings)
- **Social Proof**: Use real founder names and companies when possible
- **Claims**: Substantiate all claims with data or testimonials

### Technical Specifications
- **LinkedIn**: 1200x627px for single image, 1080x1080px for square
- **Google Display**: Responsive sizes, prioritize 300x250 and 728x90
- **YouTube**: 16:9 aspect ratio for video ads, 1280x720 minimum
- **Mobile Optimization**: All creatives must work effectively on mobile

- Double budget on highest-performing creative variants
- Expand successful campaigns to similar audiences
- Test new creative angles based on winning patterns
- Add retargeting campaigns for engaged users
