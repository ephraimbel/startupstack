# Clippie Ad Creatives - AI Generation Prompt

## Overview
Create high-converting ad creatives for Clippie across multiple platforms and formats, targeting content creators who struggle with manual clip creation and want to scale their short-form content presence.

## Brand & Positioning Context
- **Product**: AI that finds viral moments in videos and creates ready-to-post clips with captions
- **Target**: YouTubers, podcasters, streamers (10K-500K subs) producing weekly long-form content  
- **Value Prop**: "Your best moments, found and formatted" - save 4+ hours per video, increase viral potential
- **Brand Voice**: Energetic, creator-native, clever but not cocky, like a talented friend helping you succeed

## Target Audience Insights

### Primary Creators (80% of ad spend)
- **Demographics**: 22-35 years old, 65% male, English-speaking, US/UK/CA/AU
- **Pain Points**: Spending hours manually finding clips, inconsistent posting, missing viral moments, expensive editors
- **Platforms**: YouTube (main), TikTok (growing), Instagram (sporadic), Twitter (promotion)
- **Content**: Gaming, business, tech, lifestyle, education podcasts
- **Mindset**: Ambitious but overwhelmed, willing to pay for time-saving tools

### Secondary Creators (20% of ad spend)
- **Profile**: Part-time creators, course creators, business leaders doing thought leadership
- **Pain Points**: Even less time for manual work, need efficient repurposing
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn (business), YouTube (authority), Twitter (networking)

## Creative Testing Matrix

### Primary Variables to Test
1. **Hooks**: Problem-focused vs Solution-focused vs Social proof
2. **Formats**: Screen recording vs Talking head vs Animation
3. **Length**: 15s vs 30s vs 60s (where applicable)  
4. **CTAs**: "Try free" vs "Get your clips" vs "Join 12K creators"
5. **Value Props**: Time savings vs Money savings vs Growth potential

### Creative Performance Framework

#### Winning Creative Characteristics
- **Hook within 3 seconds**: Address pain point or show result immediately
- **Creator-native language**: Use terms like "viral", "algorithm", "engagement"
- **Clear before/after**: Show the transformation Clippie enables  
- **Social proof integration**: Real creator testimonials and metrics
- **No friction CTA**: Emphasize free trial, no credit card required

#### Testing Methodology
- **A/B test**: One variable at a time (hook, CTA, format)
- **Performance metrics**: CTR, CPC, conversion rate, ROAS
- **Audience segments**: Test different creator niches (gaming vs business vs lifestyle)
- **Creative refresh**: New variants weekly, retire low performers

## Creative Production Guidelines

### Visual Standards
- **Aspect Ratios**: 9:16 (TikTok/Stories), 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (Feed posts)
- **Brand Colors**: Violet (#8B5CF6) and Amber (#FBBF24) prominently featured
- **Typography**: Bold, readable fonts (Clash Display for headlines, Inter for body)
- **Logo Placement**: Subtle but visible, bottom right corner

### Video Production
- **Quality**: Professional but not overly polished (authentic creator vibe)
- **Pacing**: Quick cuts, maintain energy throughout
- **Captions**: Always include for accessibility and platform optimization
- **Audio**: Clear, energetic, trending when appropriate (TikTok)

### Performance Optimization
- **Mobile-first**: 80%+ of traffic is mobile, optimize for small screens
- **Loading speed**: Compress files appropriately for platform limits
- **Thumbnail optimization**: Eye-catching first frame for video ads
- **Call-to-action**: Clear, visible, contrasting colors
